Where I'm Going

The future

Prior to Galvanize, I've done an odd mix of business administrative and Information Technology work, with a few bits of website-related stuff thrown in here and there. I learned over time that I really enjoyed it, so I've decided to pursue it.

So, I'm excited by the opportunity to focus intenstly on learning new, relevant skills to strengthen and expand my career background.

After Galvanize, I hope to work where I can put my creativity to use while also working with directly with people to help them realize their visions.

Beyond that, I'd love to explore the possibilities of freelance work on the side, and/or other avenues where I can learn, create, and help people—ideally while being able to work freely both on my own time and where I want.
